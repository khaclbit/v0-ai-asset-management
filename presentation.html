02 Background & Problem

Assets fail before anyone is watching.

⏱ 1–1.5 min

Organizations run thousands of assets — laptops, monitors, printers, forklifts, facility equipment — across sites. Maintenance is still mostly reactive: you fix it after it breaks.

01Silent failures → unplanned downtime and expensive emergency repairs.
02Spreadsheet tracking → no single source of truth; assignments go overdue unnoticed.
03Sensor data is siloed → telemetry exists, but nobody turns it into a decision.
04Alert storms & black boxes → raw thresholds flood inboxes; autonomous AI acts without accountability.
Why today's tools fall short
CMMS / asset registries — record history, but don't predict what fails next.
Monitoring dashboards — alert on raw thresholds, causing storms; no ranking, no reasons.
Pure-ML scoring tools — output a number with no explanation and no human gate.

The gap: nothing unifies lifecycle, live telemetry, and explainable, governed decision support.

03 Proposed Solution

One governed platform: lifecycle + telemetry + AI.

⏱ 1–1.5 min

We unify asset lifecycle management with a real-time sensor pipeline and dual-AI decision support — so raw readings become ranked, explained, auditable recommendations that a human confirms.

STEP 01

Track

Every asset moves through a lifecycle state machine — one source of truth from registration to retirement.

STEP 02

Sense

MQTT streams sensor readings that are persisted, charted live, and checked against alert rules.

STEP 03

Reason

A Random Forest scores maintenance risk; an LLM explains anomalies in plain language.

STEP 04

Decide

A manager approves or defers each proposal under an SLA — logged to an immutable audit trail.

Target audience

Asset, facility & IT managers, maintenance teams, and auditors in mid-to-large organizations that run sensor-equipped fleets.

Scope of application

Any physical or IT asset that emits telemetry — from a single office to a multi-site operation. Demoed today on 5 device classes, extensible to more.
