# FLOW-03 Workflow: Maintenance and Warranty Monitoring

## Trigger Conditions

- scheduled maintenance trigger
- risk-based trigger
- warranty expiration threshold trigger

## Workflow

1. Detect maintenance or warranty trigger.
2. Route by priority and risk category.
3. Open maintenance/warranty workflow state.
4. Send notification at transition and deadline thresholds.
5. Close workflow when completion criteria satisfied.

## Notification Points

- trigger acknowledged
- approaching deadline
- overdue intervention
- completed closure
